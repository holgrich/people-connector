## Vision

An AI-powered social app that learns who you are through **fun, lightweight interaction** — like an alternative to TikTok — then connects you with people nearby who are genuinely compatible.

The core experience is entertainment first. Profile-building is a hidden side effect, never the framing.

## Three Products (in implementation order)

### Product 1 · Question Card Experience & Profile Building ← *current focus*

The app shows the user a fun, light question — hypotheticals, would-you-rathers, preference picks. The user answers by voice or text. They can skip to a new question, or open a short chat about it if they want to go deeper.

**The experience should feel like:** picking up your phone for 2 minutes of fun, not having a therapy session.

**Question types (examples):**
- Would you rather: "be an elephant without a trunk or a crocodile without teeth?"
- Preference picks: "mountains or beach?"
- Hypotheticals: "if you could only eat one cuisine for the rest of your life, what would it be?"
- Light personality reveals: "do you make plans or wing it?"

**How profiling works:** answers to fun questions reveal Big Five traits naturally. "Would you rather explore an unknown planet or throw a huge party?" tells you about both openness and extraversion without asking directly.

**Question catalog:** questions are pre-generated by Mistral and stored in Supabase. They can be reviewed before being served to users. The AI picks the next question based on which Big Five dimensions still have gaps for that user.

**Two interaction modes:**
1. **Card mode** (default): one question at a time, answer or skip, move on
2. **Chat mode** (optional, per question): tap to open a short conversation about that specific question

---

### Product 2 · Nearby People Discovery & Matching

Users can browse a pool of nearby people (target: within walking/cycling distance). Matching is based on the profiles built in Product 1.

**Key differentiator:** hyper-local. Small pool (~10 people), high compatibility signal.

---

### Product 3 · Facilitated Meeting (Future / Far Scope)

**No in-app chat.** Once matched, the app acts as a coordinator:
- Suggests a specific real-world plan based on shared interests
- Calendar integration to find shared free slots
- Weather + Google Maps for activity/location suggestions

## Technical Stack

| Layer | Choice | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| App framework | **Expo (React Native)** | TypeScript; one codebase for iOS + Android; microphone works natively |
| AI | **Mistral API** | Question generation, chat, profile extraction (`mistral-small-latest`) |
| Voice input | **Expo AV + Voxtral (Mistral STT)** | Record audio, send to Voxtral for transcription — same API key |
| Auth | **Supabase Auth** — email + password | Social login can come later |
| Backend | **Supabase** | PostgreSQL; CLI-friendly; free tier |
| Location | Device GPS via Expo Location | Only used for proximity matching in Product 2 |

### Previously explored (not chosen)
- **FlutterFlow** — microphone was a blocker; visual builder limits direct code editing
- **Firebase** — web-console-only management; NoSQL less suitable for matching queries
- **OpenAI Whisper** — replaced by Mistral Voxtral (same API key, comparable quality, cheaper)
